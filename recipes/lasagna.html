Lasagna

Lasagna is a delicious cake-like pasta made with layered sheet-like noodles, cheese, meat and tomato sauce.

Lasagna, an Italian pasta dish, is delcious and pretty easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Meat (Preferably sausage or beef)
  • Cheese
  • Oregano
  • Salt and pepper
  • Basil

How to make...

  1. Lay down noodle
  2. Layer cheese, sauce and meat
  3. Lay down noodle on top. Repeat process ad nauseam
  4. Bake in oven at 425 deg F for 30-35 minutes
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with basil
  6. Serve and enjoy
